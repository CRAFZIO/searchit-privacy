Privacy Policy Overview

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

SearchIt ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application and website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our application and services.

This Privacy Policy is designed to be transparent, user-friendly, and compliant with international privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA).

Our Commitment to Privacy

We understand that privacy is important to you. SearchIt is dedicated to protecting your personal data and maintaining your trust. We only collect information that is necessary to provide you with the best possible user experience and service.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and user profile information
  • Search Queries: The content you search for within the application
  • File Data: Information from Excel files or documents you upload
  • Feedback and Support: Messages sent through our support channels, app reviews, and customer feedback

2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, device settings
  • Usage Analytics: Features used, search patterns, application performance metrics
  • Location Data: General location information (city, country level) based on IP address
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages visited, referring URLs
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies to remember your preferences and improve your experience

3. Data from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Analytics partners and service providers
  • Social media platforms (if you use social login)
  • Other users who reference or interact with your data

Types of Data Collected

Data Type How It's Collected Purpose
Personal Identity User input during registration Account management and communication
Search Data Application usage tracking Improving search functionality and recommendations
Device Data Automatic collection from device App optimization and compatibility
Usage Analytics Analytics tools and SDKs Understanding user behavior and feature usage

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes

1. Service Delivery

We use your information to provide, maintain, and improve SearchIt services, including:

  • Processing your searches and queries
  • Managing your account and profile
  • Storing and indexing your uploaded files
  • Providing customer support

2. Communication

  • Sending service-related announcements
  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Notifying you of policy changes
  • Sending technical updates and security alerts

3. Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve features
  • Identifying and fixing bugs
  • Optimizing performance and user experience
  • Conducting research and analytics

4. Marketing and Personalization

  • Personalizing your experience based on preferences
  • Sending promotional content (with your consent)
  • Showing you relevant features and updates

5. Legal and Security

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Protecting against fraud and abuse
  • Enforcing our terms of service
  • Protecting user safety and data security

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Specific retention periods include:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active, plus 30 days after deletion
  • Search Data: Retained for 12 months for analysis purposes
  • Log Data: Retained for 6 months
  • Legal/Tax Data: Retained as required by applicable law (typically 7 years)

Data Security and Protection

Security Measures

SearchIt implements industry-leading security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption (256-bit)
  • Storage Security: Database encryption at rest using AES-256
  • Access Control: Role-based access control and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee Training: Staff trained in data protection and privacy
  • Incident Response: Dedicated security team and incident response plan

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  1. Notify affected users within 30 days
  2. Provide details about the breach and types of data compromised
  3. Recommend steps to protect your account
  4. Notify relevant regulatory authorities as required by law

Third-Party Security

We carefully vet all third-party service providers and require them to maintain adequate security measures through Data Processing Agreements (DPA).

Your Privacy Rights

Rights You Have

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

1. Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We will provide this in a commonly used, machine-readable format within 30 days.

2. Right to Rectification

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data at any time.

3. Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your personal data, except where we have a legal obligation to retain it.

4. Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your data for certain purposes.

5. Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format to transfer to another service.

6. Right to Object

You can object to marketing communications and certain types of processing.

7. Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent to process your data at any time.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address provided in the Contact section. We will respond to your request within 30 days or notify you if we need additional information.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

SearchIt does not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, email services (with DPA)
  • Legal Requirements: Law enforcement when required by court order
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

Regulatory Compliance

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

For users in the European Union, SearchIt complies with GDPR requirements including lawful basis for processing, data protection impact assessments, and privacy by design principles.

CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

For California residents, we comply with CCPA including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale of personal information.

DPDPA (Digital Personal Data Protection Act - India)

For users in India, SearchIt complies with DPDPA 2023 requirements for processing personal data with appropriate consent and security measures.

Other Jurisdictions

We comply with applicable privacy laws in all jurisdictions where we operate, including but not limited to LGPD (Brazil), PDPA (Thailand), and POPIA (South Africa).

Cookies and Tracking

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you, remember preferences, and analyze usage patterns.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential/Functional App functionality, authentication, security Session or persistent
Analytics Usage analysis, performance monitoring Typically 30 days
Preference Remembering user preferences and settings 1 year
Marketing/Advertising Personalized recommendations Up to 2 years

Managing Your Preferences

You can control cookies through your device settings or browser preferences. However, disabling functional cookies may affect app functionality.
